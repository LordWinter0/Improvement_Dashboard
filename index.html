Helpful Resources

Here are some general types of resources to help you on your journey. Tailor these to your specific interests!

For Tech Savvy & Coding:

  • Codecademy, freeCodeCamp, Coursera (online coding platforms)
  • YouTube tutorials (e.g., "Python for Beginners")
  • Local coding bootcamps or workshops

For Gaming & Strategy:

  • Chess.com, Lichess.org (for Chess)
  • Specific game communities and forums (e.g., Reddit, Discord)
  • Strategy guides and analysis videos for your chosen games

For Connect & Communicate:

  • Books on communication skills (e.g., "How to Win Friends and Influence People")
  • Toastmasters International (for public speaking)
  • Podcasts on social dynamics and conversation skills

For Organize & Streamline:

  • Productivity apps (e.g., Todoist, Notion, Trello)
  • Books on time management (e.g., "Getting Things Done")
  • Bullet Journaling guides

For Unleash Creativity:

  • Online courses (e.g., Skillshare, Domestika for art/music)
  • Prompt generators for writing or drawing
  • Local art classes or community workshops

For Physical & Mental Well-being:

  • Fitness apps (e.g., Nike Training Club, Peloton App)
  • Meditation apps (e.g., Headspace, Calm)
  • Reputable nutrition websites or dietitians
  • Yoga/Pilates studios or online classes

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I miss a day or a goal? +

It's perfectly fine! The key is consistency over time, not perfection. Just get back on track the next day. Don't let a missed day derail your entire 90-day journey.

How do I stay motivated? +

Set small, achievable daily goals. Celebrate small wins. Remind yourself of your "why" ‚Äì the vision of your glowing self. Consider finding an accountability partner.

Can I customize the pillars? +

Absolutely! This is your journey. Feel free to swap out or add pillars that resonate more with your personal growth aspirations. The goal is holistic improvement tailored to you.

When will I see results? +

Some changes, like increased energy or confidence, might appear quickly. Deeper skill acquisition and noticeable external changes will develop over the full 90 days. Trust the process!
